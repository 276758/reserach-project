FAQs


What kind of events does BPA offer?

BPA hosts competitions in a variety of categories, including finance, marketing, management, IT, and more. In addition to competitive events, BPA also offers leadership training, networking opportunities, and workshop.


What skills can I gain from BPA?

BPA helps students develop skills in areas such as leadership, public speaking, teamwork, problem-solving, and technical knowledge related to business fields. It’s also a great way to build confidence and professionalism.


How do BPA competitions work?

Students usually compete in regional competitions first. Winners advance to state competitions, and then the best in each category qualify for nationals. Events are usually individual, but there are some team events as well. Competitions can be in-person, online, or a mix of both, depending on the event and chapter.


What are the benefits of participating in an organization like BPA?

Participating in BPA helps you develop leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills; prepares you for future careers; offers networking with professionals and peers; provides opportunities to compete and win awards; and builds confidence while enhancing your resume.
